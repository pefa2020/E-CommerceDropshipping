Details

With a taste like mom's apple pie and good-for-you ingredients, this snack almost seems too good to be true. It's made with a blend of walnuts, chewy sun-dried apples, cinnamon, and sea salt and serves up 5 g of protein and 3 g of fiber.

Ingredients

Almonds, Cashews*, Maple Syrup*, Dried Apples, Sunflower Seeds*, Pumpkin Seeds*, Coconut Butter*, Coconut Nectar*, Golden Raisins*, Walnuts, Macadamia Nuts, Sesame Seed Butter, Ground Flax Seeds*, Cinnamon*, Sea Salt. *Denotes Organic Ingredients.

Allergens: Tree Nuts (Almond, Cashew, Walnut, Macadamia, Coconut), and Sesame

How it does good

Developed by a mother, it's no surprise that the YES Bar uses high-quality, clean ingredients for allergen-free snacks that both parents and children can say "yes" to. When it comes to developing relationships with their farmers, producers, and bakers, the YES Bar team never cuts corners. What they do cut is non-organic farming practices and animal products from their recipes. They're certified by the Forest Stewardship Council and continually working to keep things as close as possible to the single kitchen, small batches, and simple recipe they were founded with. They're keeping an eye on the future too and are always trying to improve, with plans to achieve carbon neutrality and develop fully compostable wrappers by 2022.

Third Party Certifications

Yes Bar

Abigail Wald created the YES bar because her son was born with so many dietary restrictions, and she was tired of saying "no" to all the foods he wanted to try. So, she made a snack he could eat: a real food snack bar that has nothing fake or added unnecessarily. Now that's something to say "yes!" to.

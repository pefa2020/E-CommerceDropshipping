Details

This rich, velvety eye cream has a winning list of ingredients. There's chaga, which is famous for easing up hyperpigmentation. Tremella, known for making the eye area noticeably smooth and hydrated. Pink snow algae, for collagen production and skin repair. And a whole load more of the good stuff, without any nasties like petrochemicals, SLS, or synthetic fragrance. Yep, the "eyes" have it. We're big fans.

Ingredients

How it does good

Many conventional skincare products on the market are made with suspect chemicals, use synthetic fragrance, and are often designed to be part of a multi-step skincare routine. But Ursa Major skincare distills it all down to the bare essentials: clean ingredients, hard-working formulas, and simple instructions. Scented with refreshing natural aromas and crafted with powerful active ingredients from the forest, their products help you achieve that "healthy mountain glow." Even if you don't live in the mountains.

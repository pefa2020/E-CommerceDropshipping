Product details

Twenty-four 500 mL (16.9 fl. oz.) Bottles

Optimize your hydration

A refreshing drinking water infused with MBO®*, a proprietary process that stabilizes oxygen in the water.

  • Uses a 15-step purification process to remove contaminants and ensure purity.
  • Is purified, remineralized, ionized, microstructured, and oxygen rich.
  • Infuses each bottle with molecular bound oxygen (MBO®*), which enhances bio-available oxygen.

FAQs

Are there vitamins in Perfect Empowered Drinking Water®*?

No.

Does Perfect Empowered Drinking Water®* contain any calories or sweeteners?

No.

Why is there sodium in Perfect Empowered Drinking Water®*?

The amount of sodium in each bottle is negligible when compared with the RDA (Recommended Daily Allowance) for adults of 2300 mg. Each bottle of Perfect Empowered Drinking Water®* has 35mg. per serving.
